# Facebook & Google Ads Strategy for Real Estate
## wesellanyhome.com Lead Generation

**Goal:** Generate qualified leads at $35-50 CPL with 5-8% lead-to-client conversion

---

## FACEBOOK LEAD GENERATION ADS (Best CPL Performance)

### Winning Messaging
**Highest Converting Headlines:**
1. "Get Your Free Home Valuation in 60 Seconds"
2. "See What Your Home Is Worth (No Agent Required)"
3. "We Buy Houses Fast—Get a Cash Offer Today"
4. "Sell Your Home As-Is, Fast"
5. "Get Out of Your House Fast—We Buy Any Condition"

### Targeting Strategy

**Core Audiences:**
- Lookalike from past sellers/cash buyers
- Interest layers:
  - "Home Improvement"
  - "Real Estate"
  - "Property Investment"
  - "Foreclosure" (where legal)

**Geographic Targeting:**
- City/ZIP code specific
- Radius targeting (15-25 miles from your service area)

**Demographic Targeting:**
- Age: 35-65 (primary homeowners)
- Income: $50k+ (can afford home repairs typically)
- Life events: Recently moved, relocated intent

**Behavioral Targeting:**
- In-market for real estate
- Home improvement interest
- Recently visited real estate websites
- Property owner targeting (use Facebook Pixel)

### Creative Formats (Ranked by Performance)

| Format | Engagement | Cost | Notes |
|--------|-----------|------|-------|
| Short Video (15-30s) | +403% | Highest ROI | Best performing format |
| Carousel (3-5 cards) | 3x clicks | High | Multiple value props |
| Image + Strong Copy | Baseline | Lowest | Still effective if well-designed |
| Video Slideshow | +42% | Low | Budget-friendly video option |

### Ad Copy Framework (Proven Template)

```
Headline: "We Buy Homes Fast—Cash Offer Today"

Primary Text:
"Don't wait for repairs. We buy homes AS-IS in [City].
✓ No realtor fees
✓ No repairs needed
✓ Fair cash offers
✓ Close in days

Get your free offer now—it takes 2 minutes."

Call to Action: "Get Free Cash Offer"
```

### Lead Form Optimization
**Critical:** Keep form to 3 fields MAX
1. Full Name
2. Email
3. Phone

**Pro Tip:** Show social proof BEFORE the form
- "1,000+ homes purchased"
- "Average offer: $X"
- "Close in 7-14 days"

**Auto-Response:** Instant SMS confirmation ("Got it! We'll call within 2 hours")

| Metric | Target | Industry Avg |
|--------|--------|---|
| Cost Per Lead (CPL) | $40-50 | $30-60 |
| Lead Form Completion Rate | 8-12% | 9.21% avg |
| Cost Per Click | $0.50-1.50 | Varies |
| Conversion Rate | 8-12% | Varies |

**Budget Allocation:**
- **Test Phase:** $500-800/week (2-3 weeks)
  - Test 3 messaging variations
  - Test audiences
  - Optimize form

  - Double down on winners

  - Expand lookalike audiences

## GOOGLE ADS SEARCH (High Intent, Immediate ROI)

### Keywords That Convert
**Primary Keywords (High Intent):**
- "We buy houses [City]"
- "Cash for homes [City]"
- "Sell my house fast [City]"
- "Home buyers near me"
- "Sell house as-is [City]"
- "Buy homes with problems"

**Secondary Keywords (Audience Building):**
- "[City] real estate investors"
- "Sell inherited house [City]"
- "Sell house in probate [City]"
- "Get cash for house [City]"

### Winning Ad Copy

**Ad #1: Direct Offer**
```
Headline 1: "We Buy Homes Fast—Cash Offer Today"
Headline 2: "No Repairs. No Commissions. Close in Days."
Headline 3: "Get Your Free Offer in Minutes"

Description 1: "We purchase homes in any condition. Fair offers. Fast closing."
Description 2: "1000+ homes bought. No agent needed. No waiting."

Display URL: wesellanyhome.com/cash-offer
Final URL: [Landing page optimized for conversion]
```

**Ad #2: Problem-Focused**
```
Headline 1: "Sell Your Home As-Is"
Headline 2: "Damage? Repairs? We Handle It All"
Headline 3: "Get a Cash Offer Today"

Description 1: "No inspections. No appraisals. Fair cash offers."
Description 2: "Close in 7-14 days. Avoid realtor fees."
```

### Landing Page Optimization (Critical for Quality Score)

**Above the Fold (First 3 seconds):**
- Headline matches ad: "We Buy Homes Fast—Cash Offer Today"
- Hero image: Home being bought
- Social proof: "Bought 1000+ homes" + star rating
- Primary CTA: "Get Free Cash Offer" (prominent button)

**Content Flow:**
1. **Unique Selling Points** (What makes you different)
   - "Fair cash offers in 24 hours"
   - "No repairs needed"
   - "No realtor fees"

2. **How It Works** (3-step simple process)
   - Step 1: Share property details
   - Step 2: Get fair cash offer
   - Step 3: Close in days

3. **Trust Signals**
   - "1000+ homes purchased"
   - Customer testimonials (with names/faces)
   - Typical offer range (e.g., "typically 85-95% of market value")
   - BBB/licensing info

4. **Lead Form**
   - Property address (or ZIP code)
   - Condition (dropdown: Excellent/Good/Needs Work)
   - Name, Email, Phone

5. **FAQ Section**
   - "How fast can you close?"
   - "How much will you offer?"
   - "Do I need to make repairs?"
   - "Will you buy my house as-is?"

### Google Ads Performance Targets

| Metric | Target | Industry |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Quality Score | 7-10/10 | 6-7 avg |
| Click-Through Rate (CTR) | 4-6% | 3.71% avg |
| Cost Per Click (CPC) | $0.80-1.50 | $2.37 avg |
| Landing Page Conversion | 8-12% | Varies |
| Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) | 8:1 | 5-6:1 typical |

### Budget & Bidding Strategy

**Initial Budget:** $1,000-2,000/month
- Bid: Target CPA ($40-50)
- Start aggressive, optimize down

**Keyword Prioritization:**
1. **High Priority (Highest Budget):** "We buy houses [City]"
2. **Medium Priority:** "Sell my house fast [City]"
3. **Testing:** Long-tail variations and problem-specific terms

### Quality Score Optimization (Improves Everything)

**Quality Score Factors:**
✓ Ad relevance (ad matches keyword)
✓ Landing page experience (mobile optimized, fast load)
✓ CTR history (compelling ads)

**How to Improve:**
- Use keyword in ad headline
- Ensure landing page loads < 3 seconds
- Test multiple ad variations
- Remove underperforming keywords

## GOOGLE LOCAL SERVICES ADS (Pay-Per-Lead Model)

**How It Works:**
- Bid on "Buy Homes" category
- Appear at top of Google search
- **Pay only when someone calls/contacts you**

**Advantages:**
- No wasted clicks
- High trust (Google verification badge)
- Lower cost structure than traditional ads

**Budget:** Start $500/month, scale based on lead quality

## VIDEO ADS (Highest Engagement)

### Video Assets to Create

**1. Property Walkthroughs (2 videos)**
- 30-60 seconds
- Show "before" condition
- Explain "why sell fast"
- Include CTA

**2. Customer Testimonials (3 videos)**
- 1-3 minutes each
- Real customers explaining their experience
- Natural/authentic (not overly produced)
- Include: name, situation, outcome

**3. Educational Content (1 video)**
- "Why Sell to a Cash Buyer?"
- Explain advantages vs. traditional sale
- Address common objections

**4. Process Explainer (1 video)**
- "How Fast Can We Close?"
- Simple 3-step walkthrough
- Set expectations

**Performance Metrics:**
- Video views: +403% more inquiries
- Video testimonials: Highest trust builder
- Average view time: Aim for >50% of video

**Budget:** Create 5-7 core videos, repurpose across platforms

## CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE (Month 1-3)

### Month 1: Test & Learn
**Facebook:**

  - "Free Valuation"
  - "Cash Offer in 24 Hours"

- $1,000/month on top-converting keywords

- CPL by campaign
- Lead form completion rate
- Phone call conversions
- SMS opt-ins

- Pause underperformers

- Implement lead scoring
- Nurture sequences for different lead types
- Calculate cost per actual sale
- Project annual CAC

## EMAIL NURTURE SEQUENCE (Critical for Conversion)

**Day 1-3:** Educational
- Why sell fast? (benefits)
- Comparison: Realtor vs. Cash buyer
- Typical timeline & process

**Day 4-7:** Social Proof
- Customer testimonials
- "1000+ homes purchased"
- Recent case studies

**Day 8-14:** Soft CTA
- "Schedule a free consultation"
- "See what your home is worth"
- Answer FAQ

**Day 15+:** Re-engagement
- Limited-time offer
- Testimonial spotlight
- Market update

**Target Open Rate:** 25%+ (real estate industry ~20%)

---

## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTOR: RESPONSE TIME

**The 5-Minute Rule:**
- Leads contacted within 5 minutes are **10x more likely to convert**
- Leads contacted after 1 hour: 10x less likely

**Implementation:**
- SMS auto-response (immediate)
- Chatbot for 24/7 initial engagement
- Phone alerting system (multiple staff can answer)
- Target: Answer phone within 2 minutes during business hours
