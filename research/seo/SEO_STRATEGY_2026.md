# Real Estate SEO Strategy 2026
## For wesellanyhome.com

**Goal:** Dominate local search for "problem properties" + cash buyer intent keywords

---

## TOP 5 SEO STRATEGIES WITH IMPLEMENTATION

### 1. Hyper-Local Long-Tail Keywords (The Zillow Killer)
**Strategy:** Compete on neighborhood-specific, intent-driven keywords Zillow ignores.

**Target Keywords:**
- "Cash home buyers in [City]"
- "We buy houses in [Neighborhood] as-is"
- "Best neighborhoods in [City] for investors"
- "Sell house fast [City] without repairs"
- "Properties needing work [City]"

**Content Implementation:**
- Create cluster pages for each neighborhood/zip code
- Link to pillar page: "Selling Your Home Fast in [Region]"
- Focus on high-intent searchers (ready to sell NOW)

**Expected Results:** 35% increase in session duration, 22% increase in qualified leads (based on case studies)

---

### 2. Pillar-Cluster Content Structure
**The Framework:**

| Pillar Page | Cluster Articles |
|------------|-----------------|
| How to Sell Your Home Fast | Selling with Damage |
| | Selling As-Is Properties |
| | Selling in Probate/Estate |
| | Selling with Tenants |
| | Selling Multiple Properties |
| | Selling Inherited Homes |
| | Selling Homes with Liens |

**Each cluster page:** 1,500-2,500 words of original content
**Linking strategy:** All clusters link to pillar; pillar links to all clusters
**Timeline:** 4-6 weeks to build complete structure
**Expected outcome:** Dominate entire "fast home sale" topic area

---

### 3. Comprehensive Neighborhood Guides + Market Data
**Content Template:**
- Average sale price by neighborhood
- Days-on-market statistics
- List-to-sale price ratios
- Top schools/amenities
- Recent market trends
- Investment opportunity analysis

**Why it works:**
- Keeps visitors on-site longer (signals to Google: authority content)
- Captures long-tail keywords
- Differentiates from Zillow's generic pages

**Volume:** 10-15 neighborhood guides minimum (go deeper than competitors)

---

### 4. Build Local Authority & Backlinks
**Backlink Strategy:**
- Partner with local mortgage lenders (link exchange)
- Get featured in local real estate news
- Partner with title companies, contractors
- Sponsor local events/organizations
- Build relationships with NAR (National Association of Realtors)

**Measurement:** Track Domain Authority + referring domains monthly

---

### 5. Continuous Monitoring & AI-Enhanced Content
**Tools & Process:**
- Weekly keyword rank tracking
- Monitor Google SERP features (PAA, featured snippets)
- Seasonal content updates (spring vs. winter selling strategies)
- Use AI for draft, apply expertise, publish

**Content Calendar:**
- Seasonal updates (quarterly minimum)
- Market condition updates (monthly)
- New property type guides (ongoing)
