## RESEARCH FINDINGS SUMMARY

### What's Working in Real Estate Right Now

1. **SEO:** Hyper-local + niche keywords dominate
   - Zillow can't win on neighborhood-specific + problem-property keywords
   - Pillar-cluster content structure = domination potential
   - Expected: 35% more engagement, 22% more leads

2. **Ads:** Video content + Facebook lead forms = highest conversion
   - Facebook CPL: $35-50 (lowest cost)
   - Google ads: Immediate high-intent traffic
   - Video ads: +403% more inquiries

3. **Positioning:** "Problem properties" niche is underexploited
   - Traditional agents avoid them
   - iBuyers lack local expertise
   - Your competitive advantage: speed + transparency + local knowledge

4. **Trust:** Transparency beats generic promises
   - Show actual numbers in case studies
   - Explain your process clearly
   - Build testimonials from real customers
