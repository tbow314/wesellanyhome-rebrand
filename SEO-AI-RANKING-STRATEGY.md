## ❓ FAQ: "How Does This Actually Get Us #1 on Google?"

**Google's Ranking Factors** (simplified):

1. **Relevance** (30%)
   - Do you mention the keyword in title, H1, content?
   - Action: Optimized titles, target keywords in content

2. **Authority** (40%)
   - Does the internet trust you as an expert?
   - Action: Backlinks, reviews, mentions in other publications

3. **User Experience** (20%)
   - Do people like your content?
   - Action: Mobile-responsive, fast loading, engaging

4. **Freshness** (10%)
   - Is your content recent/updated?
   - Action: Update neighborhood prices monthly

**For "Pittsburgh real estate agent" #1 ranking**:
- We need content about Pittsburgh real estate (relevance)
- We need backlinks + reviews showing we're trustworthy (authority)
- We need people to spend time on our site (UX)
- We need recent content (freshness)
