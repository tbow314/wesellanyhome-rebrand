Community Character

Affluent & Well-Maintained

Mt. Lebanon has approximately 33,000 residents with median household income significantly above county average. The community invests heavily in schools, parks, and infrastructure, resulting in immaculate properties and excellent municipal services.

Tree-lined streets, spacious lots (often 1+ acre), and well-maintained homes define Mt. Lebanon's character. The community has strict building and landscaping standards that preserve neighborhood quality.

Exceptional Schools

Mt. Lebanon School District is consistently ranked among Pennsylvania's best. Strong academics, excellent facilities, and engaged families make the schools a primary draw for families. High property values reflect school quality.

From elementary through high school, Mt. Lebanon schools deliver rigorous curriculum, strong college placement rates, and comprehensive extracurricular programs that attract families willing to pay premium for education.

Amenities & Lifestyle

Parks & Recreation

Mt. Lebanon operates 32 parks totaling 1,100+ acres including Jeffrey Woods Natural Area, Lakota Park, and scenic trails. Active community center offers programs for all ages. The municipality maintains pristine recreational facilities.

Location & Commute

South Hills location is close to downtown Pittsburgh (15-20 minutes), yet offers suburban peace. Easy access to major employers while maintaining residential quiet. Good highways to Pittsburgh International Airport.
