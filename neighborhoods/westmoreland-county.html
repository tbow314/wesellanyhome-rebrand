Communities We Serve in Westmoreland County

From the county seat of Greensburg to the suburban corridors of Murrysville and Penn Township, we know Westmoreland County inside and out. Explore the communities where we help families buy and sell every day.

Greensburg

The county seat. Walkable downtown, Seton Hill University, diverse housing from historic to new construction. Strong community identity.

$130K-$260K

Murrysville

Top-rated Franklin Regional schools, spacious lots, family-oriented suburban living. One of the most sought-after communities in Western PA.

$250K-$450K

Irwin

Charming small-town feel with a revitalized Main Street. Great schools, affordable homes, easy Turnpike access to Pittsburgh.

$140K-$280K

Penn Township

Growing suburban hub adjacent to Irwin. Penn-Trafford School District, newer developments, shopping and dining along Route 30.

Monroeville (Border)

Straddles the Allegheny-Westmoreland line. Major retail corridor, Gateway School District, excellent highway connectivity.

Latrobe

Home of Saint Vincent College and the Pittsburgh Steelers training camp. Rich history, tight-knit neighborhoods, and affordable living.

Jeannette

Former glass manufacturing center with revitalization underway. Entry-level pricing makes it attractive for first-time buyers and investors.

New Kensington

Allegheny River community experiencing renewal. Affordable homes, growing arts scene, and proximity to both Pittsburgh and Westmoreland suburbs.

Why Westmoreland County?

Affordable Family Communities

Median home prices well below the national average. Families get more house, more land, and more value than comparable Pittsburgh suburbs without sacrificing quality of life.

Strong School Districts

Franklin Regional, Penn-Trafford, Hempfield, and Greater Latrobe consistently rank among the top school districts in Western Pennsylvania. Families relocate here specifically for education quality.

Easy Pittsburgh Commute

Route 30 and the Pennsylvania Turnpike provide direct access to Downtown Pittsburgh in 30-45 minutes. Live in Westmoreland County, work anywhere in the metro region.

Suburban & Rural Mix

Whether you want a walkable suburban neighborhood in Murrysville or acreage outside Latrobe, Westmoreland County offers lifestyle options that Allegheny County simply cannot match.
