Mario A. Rudolph is the owner and founder of We Sell Any Home, a top-producing real estate team operating under Howard Hanna Real Estate Services in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Mario has closed more than 173 transactions across western Pennsylvania and holds a perfect 5.0 Zillow rating across 17 verified reviews — one of the highest sustained ratings for any Pittsburgh realtor across the Allegheny County market.

Mario earned Howard Hanna’s Multi-Million Dollar Producer designation through consistent top-decile sales production. His clients range from first-time buyers in Canonsburg and McMurray to move-up families in Mt. Lebanon and Upper St. Clair, to investors assembling rental portfolios across Allegheny and Washington counties. The common thread across every engagement is a hands-on, family-run approach: clients work directly with Mario from first showing through closing table, not with a rotating cast of junior agents.

As a Pittsburgh native, Mario brings deep market knowledge of the neighborhoods where his clients buy and sell — Mt. Lebanon, Squirrel Hill, Shadyside, Fox Chapel, Cranberry Township, Peters Township, Upper St. Clair, Bethel Park, Canonsburg, and McMurray. He understands the price-per-square-foot dynamics by school district, the seasonality of inventory across Washington and Butler counties, and the competitive strategies required to win offers in the tight-inventory submarkets that define the 2026 Pittsburgh housing cycle.
