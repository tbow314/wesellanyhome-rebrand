Mario P. Rudolph is a licensed Pittsburgh realtor with We Sell Any Home at Howard Hanna Real Estate Services. He holds a Master of Education (M.Ed.) from California University of Pennsylvania and brings the teaching skill set — clear communication, patient explanation, and structured walkthroughs — directly into the real estate transaction. For first-time buyers and clients who have not purchased in a decade or more, that educational background is an unusual and genuine advantage.

Mario specializes in buyer representation and first-time homebuyer guidance across Allegheny and Washington counties. His workflow with new buyers follows a clear four-phase structure: pre-approval and budget clarification, neighborhood strategy and price-per-square-foot benchmarking, offer and inspection execution, and closing preparation with a hand-off to ongoing homeowner resources. Clients come out of the process understanding why they made each decision — not just that it got made.

As part of the We Sell Any Home team, Mario works alongside Mario A. Rudolph (Owner & Founder) and Julie DiLucia (Co-Owner) to deliver the family-run, highly responsive service experience the team is known for across the Pittsburgh metro. Clients are never handed off to a junior transaction coordinator — they work directly with a licensed realtor from first showing through closing table.
