Julie DiLucia is the co-owner and co-founder of We Sell Any Home, a family-run real estate team operating under Howard Hanna Real Estate Services in the Pittsburgh metro area. Julie partners with Mario A. Rudolph to serve buyers and sellers across Allegheny County, Washington County, Westmoreland County, and Butler County, delivering the kind of attentive, highly responsive client experience that only comes from a tightly-held family practice.

Julie’s client focus is first-time buyers, move-up families, and sellers navigating life transitions — downsizing, relocation, estate sales, and cross-county moves within western Pennsylvania. She specializes in walking clients through the full transaction cycle: pricing strategy and staging on the listing side, pre-approval and multi-offer strategy on the buyer side, and the detail-heavy middle phase of inspections, appraisals, and title coordination that too often derails deals for less-organized teams.

As a Pittsburgh-area realtor with Howard Hanna Real Estate Services, Julie operates inside the largest independently-owned real estate broker in the United States — giving We Sell Any Home clients access to Howard Hanna’s buyer-side tools, exclusive listings network, and back-office transaction support while preserving the family-owned boutique experience that defines the team.
