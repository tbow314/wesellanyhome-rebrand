## SEO Optimization Checklist for Each Guide

- [ ] Title tag includes "[Neighborhood] homes for sale Pittsburgh"

- [ ] H1 = "[Neighborhood] Pittsburgh: Homes, Prices & Lifestyle"
- [ ] At least 1,500 words of content
- [ ] Internal links to:
  - Main homepage
  - Buying/selling guides
  - Free calculator
  - Contact form
- [ ] Images of actual neighborhood (own photos or licensed)
- [ ] Real data (prices from Zillow, schools from GreatSchools)

## Data Sources for Accuracy

When filling in the template, use these sources:

- **Home prices**: Zillow, Redfin, Zillow API
- **Schools**: GreatSchools.org, Pittsburgh Public Schools
- **Demographics**: Census.gov, City-Data.com
- **Restaurants/Attractions**: Google Maps, Yelp, local guides
- **Local news**: Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, PittsburghCityPaper
- **Historical data**: Your own sales records

## Time to Create One Guide

- Research: 30 minutes
- Writing: 45 minutes
- Images/formatting: 15 minutes
- Optimization: 15 minutes
- **Total**: ~2 hours per guide

**For 15 guides**: ~30 hours of work, but worth 100+ monthly organic visits + 10-20 warm leads per month.

## Distribution Strategy

Once you create a neighborhood guide:

1. **Email** - Send to past clients who live in that neighborhood
2. **LinkedIn** - Share with caption: "Just published our [Neighborhood] Pittsburgh guide!"
3. **Facebook** - Local Pittsburgh real estate groups
4. **Zillow** - Link to guide from your profile
5. **Local directories** - Pittsburgh neighborhood blogs, lifestyle sites
6. **Google Search Console** - Submit new URL for indexing

Each share = potential backlink = ranking boost.
